Background

I am originally from a small country in the Caribbean called Dominican Republic, and I moved to the U.S.A when I was 10 years old. Computer Science piqued my interest as a kid with playing games such as Call of Duty or Assassin's Creed, but it wasn't until my first year of college that my passion for Computing surged and until one of my friends told me to, that I actually started to consider it as a career option!

Hobbies

Besides loving to consume mass media like Netflix and Youtube, I like watching anime and dissecting albums, and do occasional HackerRank and LeetCode Problems to sharpen my DSA skills. I also like hanging out with friends in mostly any setting, and I'm a firm believer that laughter is the best medicine.

Goals

Projects & Courses

A collection of the projects and courses I've worked on and had the priviledge to completete during my time at Georgia Tech!

Spotify Wrapper

A project organized by me and 6 members featuring a complete revamp of Spotify Wrapped. You can use your Spotify login to access it and all of your info will sync!

College Scheduler

A 3 person project where we developed an app to match every college student's needs! Including class scheduling and assignments as well as a to-do list and calendar.

A mini game developed using a game boy advance container where you play against the computer in a game of Pong.

Computer Architecture & Programming

A course where I learned pivotal concepts in computing such as introduction to the processor and its components as well as languages such as Assembly and C.

One of the most important courses I have taken covering data structures such as Lists, Trees, Graphs, etc. In addition to algorithms like Merge Sort and Djikstra's Shortest Path.

A more recent course I have taken, covering important concepts such as processor design and instruction set design, in addition to abstractions like memory managment and process scheduling.
